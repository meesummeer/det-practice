ℹ️ About the real DET format

The Duolingo English Test is adaptive and takes about 1 hour. Scores range from 10 to 160.

  • Adaptive: Read & Select, Fill in the Blanks, Read & Complete
  • Writing: Write About Photo, Interactive Writing, Writing Sample
  • Interactive Reading: Complete sentences/passage, Highlight, Main Idea, Title
  • Speaking: Read Aloud, Speak About Photo, Read Then Speak (not in this practice app)

This simulator focuses on reading & writing types you can practice without AI speaking judges.
